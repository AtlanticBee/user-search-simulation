# Data Sources

Since this is not a strictly official project, I have collected a number of articles, Office of National Statistics (ONS) data sets, and also where convenient best-guesses.

By folder, the sources are as follows:

Age - ONS
Calendar - Manual
England Region - ONS
Leisure Activities - Google Search and manual removal of terms by age group (e.g. no gambling for children)
Mood - names are from the Hoffman Institute. I manually removed duplicates and focused on only negative and positive moods. Percentages for neutral, good and bad were from a quick Gemini query.
Name - ONS (data sets are by decade according to the closest census for each decade)
Occupation - Best Guess
Settlements - Best Guess
Waking Hour - Gemini query (also helped by generating the cumulative probabilities)
